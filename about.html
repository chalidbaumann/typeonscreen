Space 1.8 is Nala Sinephro's debut album. Together with a variety of local jazz musicians she truly created something special.
The album consists of 8 tracks, which in themselfs are unique. But together they introduce you to a hole new experience. You will marvel at how well harmony and spontanity to together.
The tracks, which range from just over one minute up to about 18 minutes, fuse jazz balladry and beat music together and create an incomparable abience.
